Short Biography

Dr. Hailun Lin is the Associate Professor at the Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences (IIE, CAS). She obtained her Ph.D. from the Institute of Computational Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ICT, CAS) in 2015. She have published more than 30 journal/conference papers.

Research Interests

Big Data Analysis, Knowledge Computing
